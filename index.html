Answer Smash

Based on Richard Osman's House of Games

How to Play

You'll be shown a trivia question and a picture.

The answer to the question overlaps with the name of what's in the picture.

Smash them together to get the answer!

Example

Q: "Which London Underground line is represented in grey on the Tube map?"

Picture: Lee Mack

Trivia answer: Jubilee

Answer Smash: Jubilee Mack
